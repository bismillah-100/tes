Privacy Policy for Maktabah

Last updated: April 3, 2026

Maktabah is developed and maintained by Yayasan Darun Najah Keboncandi.

No Data Collection Maktabah does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All data generated by the app — including annotations, search history, library configurations, and reading preferences — is stored locally on your device and never leaves it.

No Analytics or Tracking Maktabah does not use any analytics, crash reporting, or tracking services. We have no visibility into how you use the app.

No Third-Party Services Maktabah does not connect to any third-party servers. Book downloads are fetched directly from GitHub Releases and stored locally on your device.

No Account Required Maktabah does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information.

Children's Privacy Maktabah does not knowingly collect any information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: Yayasan Darun Najah Keboncandi admin@darunnajahkeboncandi.my.id
